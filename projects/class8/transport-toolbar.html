Transport Toolbar

The following buttons are present in this toolbar.

Button Action
Pause Button Will pause during playback or recording. To play press this button again.
Play Button Press the play button to listen to the selected audio in your project.
Stop Button Press to stop button or press spacebar to stop playback immediately.
Skip to Start Button Press to move the playback head to start of the track.
Skip to End Button Press to move the playback head to end of track.
Record Button Press to record button to record a new track a new track from your computer's sound input device.
